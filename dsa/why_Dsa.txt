# Why Are Data Structure and Algorithm is Important?
-Because how you organize your data completely changes how fast your computer can use it. If you organize it poorly, your apps run slow, freeze, or crash. 
   If you organize it right, everything feels instant.
ex: Data structures are important because they turn a chaotic pile of information into an organized system, 
     allowing your software to find, add, or change data in milliseconds.

-Without the right data structure, Google Maps would take hours to load a route, Instagram wouldn't be able to fetch your feed, 
  and video games would lag to a standstill.
